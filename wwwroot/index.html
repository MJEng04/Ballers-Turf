Latest News

Read the newest updates, stories, and event coverage from Baller's Turf.

Ballers Turf Partners With Local Schools to Promote Youth Football

Ballers Turf has teamed up with several schools in the area to support grassroots football development. The partnership will provide free coaching sessions, access to training facilities, and opportunities for young players to take part in organised matches throughout the season.

Ballers Turf Rolls Out Free Open Play Sundays for the Community

Ballers Turf is launching "Open Play Sundays," a new initiative offering free access to the AstroTurf pitch for the month. Players of all ages are welcome to drop in for casual matches, practice sessions, or friendly kickabouts in a relaxed and inclusive environment for a limited time.

Local Derby to Take Place at Ballers Turf This Weekend

Highly anticipated local derby scheduled for this weekend at Ballers Turf, drawing fans and players from across the community. Both teams are preparing for an intense, entertaining match as they battle for bragging rights on our newly installed pitch.

Our Facilities

A glimpse at what our venue has to offer

Full-Size 4G AstroTurf Pitch

Our premium 4G AstroTurf pitch is where the magic happens - ready for those top-bins moments you swear weren't a fluke. Rain or shine, our surface stays match-ready, so you can focus on skills, last-minute winners, and proving your mate wrong about who's really the star of the show.

❮ ❯

Player Changing Rooms & Showers

Prepare like a pro in our modern changing rooms - spacious, clean, and perfect for pre-match feels and post-match debriefs. Finish strong with hot showers that feel even better after a muddy sliding tackle or a dramatic extra-time brace.

❮ ❯

Refreshments & Social Area

Whether you're celebrating a win, recovering from an undeserved loss, or just pretending you're the chosen one, our modern social area has you covered. Grab a drink, vibe with your mates, talk football, or plan your next big fixture - it's the ultimate cooldown area off the pitch.
